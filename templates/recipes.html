Breakfast
Overnight Oats with Berries

A nutritious and convenient breakfast packed with fiber and antioxidants.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tsp honey
Instructions:
  1. Mix oats, milk, yogurt, and chia seeds
  2. Refrigerate overnight
  3. Top with berries and honey
  4. Enjoy cold in the morning
5 min 250 cal
Smoothie
Green Power Smoothie

Energizing smoothie loaded with vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1/2 cup pineapple
  • 1 tbsp flax seeds
Instructions:
  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add ice if desired
  4. Serve immediately
5 min 200 cal

Snack
Energy Balls

No-bake snacks perfect for pre or post-workout energy boost.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup dates, pitted
  • 1 cup almonds
  • 2 tbsp cacao powder
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions:
  1. Blend all ingredients in food processor
  2. Roll into small balls
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes
  4. Store in airtight container
15 min 120 cal
Snack
Hummus & Veggies

Classic healthy snack with protein-rich hummus and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients:
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Assorted veggie sticks
Instructions:
  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon
  2. Add olive oil while blending
  3. Season with salt and paprika
  4. Serve with carrot, celery, cucumber

Nutrition Tips

Do's
  • Eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean proteins in every meal
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • Plan and prepare meals in advance
  • Eat mindfully and without distractions
Don'ts
  • Avoid excessive processed foods
  • Limit added sugars and artificial sweeteners
  • Don't skip meals, especially breakfast
  • Avoid eating late at night
  • Minimize trans fats and excessive sodium
  • Don't rely on fad diets
