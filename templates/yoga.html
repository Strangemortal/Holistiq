Yoga & Mindfulness

Hatha Yoga

A gentle introduction to basic yoga postures. Perfect for beginners.

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Pace: Slow
  • Level: Beginner
Key Poses:
  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
  • Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  • Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)
  • Child's Pose (Balasana)
Vinyasa Yoga

Dynamic flow connecting breath with movement. More challenging and energetic.

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Pace: Moderate-Fast
  • Level: Intermediate
Key Poses:
  • Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)
  • Plank Pose (Phalakasana)
  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)
  • Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)
Restorative Yoga

Relaxing practice using props to support the body in restful poses.

  • Duration: 60-75 minutes
  • Pace: Very Slow
  • Level: All Levels
Key Poses:
  • Supported Child's Pose
  • Legs-Up-The-Wall (Viparita Karani)
  • Reclining Bound Angle
  • Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

Benefits: Reduces stress, lowers blood pressure, improves core muscle stability

How to practice:

  1. Sit or lie down comfortably
  2. Place one hand on your chest and one on your belly
  3. Breathe in slowly through your nose, letting your belly expand
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth
  5. Repeat for 5-10 minutes

Benefits: Balances left and right brain, reduces anxiety, improves focus

How to practice:

  1. Sit comfortably with a straight spine
  2. Close your right nostril with your thumb
  3. Inhale through your left nostril
  4. Close left nostril, open right, exhale through right
  5. Inhale through right, switch, exhale through left
  6. Repeat for 5-10 rounds

Benefits: Calms nervous system, improves concentration, reduces stress

How to practice:

  1. Inhale for 4 counts
  2. Hold breath for 4 counts
  3. Exhale for 4 counts
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts
  5. Repeat for 5-10 minutes
