Exercise Tips

  • Warm up properly: Always start with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Listen to your body: If something hurts, stop and rest.
  • Be consistent: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week.
  • Cool down: End with 5-10 minutes of stretching to prevent injury.
